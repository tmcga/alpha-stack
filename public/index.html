How it works.

Three steps.

1. Pick a tool.

Browse 25 calculators grouped by desk — Valuation, Options, Fixed Income, Portfolio, M&A, Real Estate, VC.

2. Enter inputs.

Each field has a hint. Click "example" to pre-fill realistic numbers. Optional fields are marked.

3. Get answers.

Results render as a card with key metrics, a breakdown table, and a copy button. Share the URL to share the analysis.
