# Pitch Deck: Series B Climate Tech Startup

## Presentation Type
Investor pitch — Series B fundraise

## Company Profile
- **Name:** CarbonLock (fictional)
- **Industry:** Climate tech — industrial carbon capture as a service
- **Stage:** Series B ($40M target raise)
- **Revenue:** $12M ARR, growing 120% YoY
- **Gross Margin:** 55% (hardware + software; software-only is 82%)
- **Customers:** 35 industrial facilities (cement, steel, chemical plants)
- **Team:** 85 employees, founded by 2 MIT chemical engineering PhDs
- **Prior Funding:** $8M seed (Lowercarbon Capital), $22M Series A (Breakthrough Energy Ventures)

## Audience
Growth-stage climate/deep-tech VCs (Prelude Ventures, Congruent Ventures, DCVC) and strategic investors (industrial gas companies)

- **Unit Economics:** $180K average contract value, 18-month payback, 3.5x LTV/CAC
- **Technology Moat:** Proprietary sorbent material with 40% better capture efficiency than competitors; 3 patents granted, 7 pending
- **Market Size:** $15B addressable by 2030 (industrial carbon capture), $120B total carbon management
- **Use of Funds:** 50% manufacturing scale-up, 30% R&D (next-gen sorbent), 20% go-to-market
