# Board Deck: Quarterly Board Update for Growth-Stage SaaS

## Report Type
Quarterly board update

## Audience
Board of directors — 2 VC partners (lead investors), 2 independent directors (former SaaS CEOs), CEO, CFO

## Company Profile
- **Name:** SignalPath (fictional)
- **Industry:** B2B SaaS — revenue intelligence platform
- **Stage:** Series B ($35M raised total), 2 years post-funding
- **ARR:** $18M (was $12M same quarter last year = 50% YoY growth)
- **Employees:** 140
- **Runway:** 18 months at current burn

## Quarterly Performance
| KPI | Q4 Actual | Q4 Target | vs Target | Q3 Actual | YoY |
|-----|-----------|-----------|-----------|-----------|-----|
| ARR | $18.0M | $17.5M | +3% | $16.2M | +50% |
| Net New ARR | $1.8M | $1.5M | +20% | $1.4M | +38% |
| Gross Margin | 77% | 78% | -1pp | 76% | +3pp |
| Net Revenue Retention | 118% | 120% | -2pp | 121% | -3pp |
| CAC Payback (months) | 14 | 12 | +2mo | 13 | +2mo |
| Burn Multiple | 1.8x | 1.5x | +0.3x | 2.1x | improved |
| Headcount | 140 | 145 | -5 | 128 | +35% |

## Key Topics for Board Discussion
1. **Beat on ARR** but NRR declining — expansion revenue slowing, churn ticking up
2. **CAC payback stretching** — marketing efficiency declining as we move upmarket
3. **5 unfilled roles** — VP Engineering search stalled (3 months, 2 rejected offers)
4. **Product:** Shipped AI summarization feature — early adoption at 40% of users
5. **Competitive:** Two new entrants raised $20M+ each in last quarter
6. **Strategic question:** Should we accelerate into enterprise (longer sales cycles, higher ACV) or double down on mid-market velocity?

## Reporting Cadence
Quarterly
