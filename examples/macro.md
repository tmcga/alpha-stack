# Global Macro: European Rate Divergence Trade

## Scope
Multi-region — Europe vs. US

## Asset Class Focus
Cross-asset: rates, FX, equities

## Time Horizon
3-12 month tactical view

## Macro Thesis
ECB is about to diverge from the Fed. Euro area inflation is at 2.1% and falling, while US core PCE is stuck at 3.2%. The ECB will cut 100bps over the next 12 months while the Fed holds. This divergence is not fully priced — EUR/USD forwards imply only 50bps of differential widening.
