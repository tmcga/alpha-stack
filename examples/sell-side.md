# Sell-Side M&A: Specialty Chemical Company

## Company Profile
- **Name:** SpecChem Industries (fictional)
- **Industry:** Specialty chemicals — adhesives & sealants
- **Revenue:** $220M (LTM), growing 7% organically
- **EBITDA:** $62M (28.2% margin), up from 26% two years ago
- **Capex:** $12M/year (maintenance) + $8M growth capex winding down
- **Net Debt:** $145M (2.3x leverage)
- **Ownership:** Family-owned, second generation, 100% equity held by family trust
- **Employees:** 850 across 4 manufacturing plants (US Southeast)

## Process Parameters
- **Process Stage:** Pre-launch — preparing marketing materials
- **Process Type:** Targeted auction (15-20 buyers)
- **Seller Objectives:** Maximize price, retain key management team (CEO stays 2 years), close within 6 months
- **Key Constraints:** Two customers represent 28% of revenue (concentration risk). Environmental remediation reserve of $4M at one plant. Family wants clean exit — no earnouts.
