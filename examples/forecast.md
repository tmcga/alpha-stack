# Forecast: 13-Week Cash Flow for Growth Company

## Workflow Type
13-week cash flow forecast (liquidity management)

## Company Profile
- **Name:** FleetPulse (fictional)
- **Industry:** Fleet management SaaS + hardware (IoT sensors)
- **Revenue:** $28M ARR software + $8M hardware (one-time sales)
- **Monthly Cash Burn:** $450K (improving from $650K six months ago)
- **Cash on Hand:** $4.2M
- **Debt:** $3M term loan ($75K monthly payments)
- **Stage:** Series B, considering bridge round or Series C

## Weekly Cash Flow Drivers
| Category | Weekly Average | Variability | Notes |
|----------|---------------|-------------|-------|
| Software Collections | $540K | Low (recurring) | 85% annual contracts, billed monthly |
| Hardware Sales | $155K | High (lumpy) | Large orders come quarterly |
| Payroll | -$380K | Low | Bi-weekly, 95 employees |
| AWS/Infrastructure | -$42K | Low | Monthly bill, paid weekly allocation |
| Hardware COGS | -$85K | High (matches sales) | Component costs + manufacturing |
| Sales Commissions | -$35K | Medium | Monthly, but quarterly accelerators |
| Rent & Facilities | -$28K | None | Fixed lease |
| Professional Services | -$15K | Low | Legal, accounting |
| Debt Service | -$19K | None | Fixed weekly allocation |
| **Net Weekly Cash Flow** | **+$91K** | | Positive but thin |

## Key Events in 13-Week Window
- **Week 3:** Large hardware order expected ($420K, 70% probability)
- **Week 5:** Quarterly sales commission accelerators due ($180K)
- **Week 7:** Annual insurance premium renewal ($95K)
- **Week 9:** Major customer renewal — if lost, -$35K/week ongoing
- **Week 11:** Potential Series C term sheet (could add $15M+)

## Purpose
Board and existing investors want visibility on runway. Need to answer: "How many weeks until we hit $2M cash floor (minimum operating balance)?"

## Update Cadence
Weekly rolling update
