# Private Capital: Software Buyout

## Stage
Growth equity / buyout

## Fund Economics
- **Fund:** Apex Growth Partners Fund IV (fictional)
- **Fund Size:** $2.5B
- **Target Check Size:** $150-300M equity
- **Dry Powder:** $1.4B remaining
- **Investment Period:** Year 2 of 5
- **Target Net IRR:** 20%+
- **Target MOIC:** 2.5x+

## Target Company
- **Name:** DataStream Analytics (fictional)
- **Industry:** Vertical SaaS — supply chain analytics for CPG companies
- **Revenue:** $85M ARR, growing 28%
- **Gross Margin:** 80%
- **EBITDA Margin:** 22% ($18.7M)
- **Rule of 40:** 50 (28% growth + 22% margin)
- **Net Revenue Retention:** 125%
- **Customers:** 180 enterprise accounts, $472K average ACV
- **Logo Churn:** 4% annually
- **Team:** 320 employees, founded 2017

## Deal Terms Under Discussion
- **Enterprise Value:** $700M (8.2x ARR, 37.4x EBITDA)
- **Equity Check:** $250M (Apex leads)
- **Leverage:** 3.5x EBITDA ($65M debt)
- **Management Rollover:** 15% of equity (CEO + CTO rolling)
- **Co-Investors:** None (solo deal)

## Investment Thesis
CPG supply chain is a $50B software TAM growing 18%. DataStream has a unique data moat — 8 years of SKU-level velocity data across 40% of US grocery chains. No credible competitor has this dataset. Path to $200M+ ARR in 3-4 years through land-and-expand within existing customer base (125% NRR) + adjacent verticals (pharma, industrial).
