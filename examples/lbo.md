# LBO: Enterprise Cloud Security Company

## Transaction Parameters
- **Target:** CyberVault Systems (fictional)
- **Enterprise Value:** $2.0B (12x LTM EBITDA)
- **LTM EBITDA:** $167M
- **LTM Revenue:** $580M
- **Existing Debt:** $200M (to be refinanced)
- **Transaction Fees:** $35M (advisory + financing)
- **Minimum Cash:** $25M

|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| Revenue Growth | 10% | 9% | 8% | 7% | 6% |
| EBITDA Margin | 29.5% | 30.5% | 31.5% | 32.0% | 32.5% |
| D&A (% Rev) | 3.0% | 3.0% | 2.8% | 2.8% | 2.5% |
| Capex (% Rev) | 4.0% | 3.5% | 3.5% | 3.0% | 3.0% |
| NWC Change ($M) | -8 | -7 | -6 | -5 | -5 |
| Tax Rate | 25% | 25% | 25% | 25% | 25% |
| SBC (% Rev) | 2.0% | 1.8% | 1.5% | 1.5% | 1.2% |

## Capital Structure
- **Total Leverage:** 5.5x EBITDA ($918M total debt)
- **Term Loan B:** $650M at SOFR + 375bps, 1% annual amortization
- **Second Lien:** $168M at SOFR + 650bps, bullet
- **Revolver:** $100M undrawn at SOFR + 325bps
- **Financing Fees:** 2.0% OID on TLB, 3.0% on second lien
- **Cash Sweep:** 50% of excess cash flow above $25M minimum

## Exit Assumptions
- **Hold Period:** 5 years
- **Exit Multiple:** 13x EBITDA (1 turn of expansion)
- **Exit Method:** Strategic sale
