# Merger Arbitrage: Cash & Stock Deal in Healthcare

## Event Type
Merger — definitive agreement signed

## Deal Structure
- **Acquirer:** GlobalPharma Inc. (fictional), $120B market cap
- **Target:** BioSignal Therapeutics (fictional), $8.5B pre-deal market cap
- **Consideration:** Mixed — $42 cash + 0.15 shares of GlobalPharma per share
- **GlobalPharma Current Price:** $285
- **Implied Deal Value:** $42 + (0.15 x $285) = $84.75 per target share
- **Target Current Price:** $81.20 (spread: $3.55 / 4.2%)
- **Pre-Deal Target Price:** $62.00

## Timeline & Regulatory
- **Announcement Date:** 3 weeks ago
- **Expected Close:** 6-8 months (FTC second request likely)
- **Regulatory Jurisdiction:** US (FTC), EU (Phase I filed)
- **Overlap Concern:** Both companies have competing oncology diagnostics platforms; FTC may require divestiture of one
- **Shareholder Vote:** Target shareholder approval required (50% threshold)
- **Target Board Recommendation:** Unanimous approval
- **Termination Fee:** $425M (5% of deal value) — target pays if walks

## Risk Assessment
- **FTC Risk:** Medium — diagnostics overlap is real but narrow (2 of 15 product lines)
- **Financing Risk:** Low — acquirer has $18B cash + committed bridge facility
- **MAE Risk:** Low — target has no pending litigation or regulatory issues
- **Topping Bid Risk:** Low — deal price is 37% premium; no other logical strategic buyers

## Portfolio Context
- **Fund Strategy:** Event-driven / merger arb
- **Fund AUM:** $3B
- **Position Size Limit:** 5% of NAV
- **Hedge Ratio:** Typically hedge 80-100% of stock component
