# Budget: SaaS Company Annual Budget Build

## Workflow Type
Annual budget build (bottom-up)

## Company Profile
- **Name:** Relay Ops (fictional)
- **Industry:** B2B SaaS — workflow automation for operations teams
- **Revenue:** $32M ARR (current), target $48M ARR (end of budget year = 50% growth)
- **Employees:** 180 (plan to reach 260 by year-end)
- **Fiscal Year:** January - December
- **Stage:** Series C, $22M cash remaining, not yet profitable

## Budget Year Targets (Board-Approved)
- **Revenue:** $48M ARR exit / $40M recognized revenue
- **Gross Margin:** 78% (up from 75% — infrastructure optimization)
- **Operating Loss:** -$8M (down from -$14M, path to breakeven)
- **Cash Burn:** -$6M (ending cash: $16M, 20+ months runway)
- **Headcount:** 260 (net +80 hires)

## Departmental Structure
| Department | Current HC | Planned HC | Current Budget | Key Drivers |
|------------|-----------|------------|----------------|------------|
| Engineering | 65 | 90 | $12.0M | Product roadmap, infrastructure |
| Sales | 40 | 60 | $7.5M | Quota-carrying reps, OTE structure |
| Marketing | 18 | 28 | $4.2M | Pipeline generation, brand, events |
| Customer Success | 22 | 35 | $3.8M | Onboarding, retention, expansion |
| G&A (Finance, HR, Legal) | 15 | 20 | $3.5M | Compliance, recruiting |
| Product | 12 | 15 | $2.0M | Design, research, analytics |
| Executive | 8 | 12 | $3.0M | Leadership hires (VP Eng, CRO) |

## Key Assumptions
- **Average Fully Loaded Cost:** Engineering $185K, Sales $165K (OTE), Marketing $145K, CS $130K, G&A $140K
- **Benefits Load:** 25% of base salary
- **Hiring Ramp:** Average start date Q1 mid-month, Q2 early, Q3/Q4 backloaded
- **Quota Ramp:** New AEs productive at 50% in month 3, 75% in month 4, 100% in month 6
- **Infrastructure:** AWS spend growing from $180K/month to $220K/month (scale + optimization offset)

## Data Available
- Last 2 years of monthly P&L by department
- Hiring plan by role and start month
- Sales capacity model (AEs x quota x attainment)
- Contract-level revenue schedule (existing ARR)
