# Credit Analysis: High Yield Issuer Under Pressure

## Credit Quality Tier
High yield (B2/B rated)

## Issuer Profile
- **Name:** Cascade Media Holdings (fictional)
- **Industry:** Digital media & advertising technology
- **Revenue:** $680M (flat YoY, previously growing 15%)
- **EBITDA:** $102M (15% margin, down from $145M / 19% two years ago)
- **Free Cash Flow:** $18M (down from $72M)
- **Total Debt:** $820M (8.0x leverage, up from 5.7x)
- **Cash:** $45M

## Instrument Under Analysis
- **Bond:** 7.75% Senior Unsecured Notes due 2029
- **Outstanding:** $400M
- **Current Price:** $72.50 (yield: 14.8%)
- **Rating:** B2/B (both agencies have negative outlook)
- **Comparable Spread:** CCC index at +850bps, B index at +450bps

## Capital Structure
| Tranche | Amount | Rate | Maturity | Leverage |
|---------|--------|------|----------|----------|
| Revolver | $75M ($30M drawn) | SOFR+350 | 2027 | 0.3x |
| First Lien TL | $390M | SOFR+425 | 2028 | 4.1x |
| Senior Unsecured Notes | $400M | 7.75% | 2029 | 8.0x |

## Key Financial Trends
- Revenue growth stalled as programmatic ad market shifted to AI-driven platforms
- Customer churn accelerated: net retention dropped from 105% to 88%
- Capex investment in AI platform: $35M this year (up from $15M)
- Management guided to EBITDA recovery in 18 months but Street is skeptical

## Investment Direction
Evaluating as a **long** (distressed value) — the bond at $72.50 implies ~30% default probability, but we think recovery value in a restructuring would be $55-65 and the business can stabilize.
