# Restructuring: Distressed Retail Chain

## Company Profile
- **Name:** Harbor Retail Group (fictional)
- **Industry:** Specialty retail — home furnishings
- **Revenue:** $1.8B (down from $2.3B three years ago)
- **EBITDA:** $45M (2.5% margin, down from $230M / 10%)
- **Employees:** 12,000 across 340 stores
- **Status:** Evaluating restructuring alternatives; has not yet filed

## Capital Structure
| Tranche | Amount | Rate | Maturity | Security | Covenants |
|---------|--------|------|----------|----------|-----------|
| ABL Revolver | $180M drawn / $250M committed | SOFR+200 | Jun 2027 | First lien on inventory + receivables | Borrowing base formula |
| First Lien Term Loan | $650M | SOFR+450 | Mar 2028 | First lien on all assets (ex-ABL priority) | 4.5x total leverage (in breach) |
| Senior Unsecured Notes | $400M | 8.25% fixed | Sep 2028 | Unsecured | Limited covenants |
| Subordinated Notes | $150M | 10.5% fixed | Dec 2029 | Unsecured, subordinated | None |

## Liquidity & Distress Indicators
- **Cash:** $35M
- **ABL Availability:** $70M additional (borrowing base shrinking with inventory)
- **Liquidity Runway:** ~4 months at current burn rate ($25M/month cash burn)
- **Covenant Breach:** Total leverage covenant breached last quarter (actual 8.2x vs. 4.5x limit); waiver expires in 60 days
- **Near-Term Maturities:** None until 2027, but covenant breach triggers cross-default risk
- **Store Performance:** 120 of 340 stores are cash-flow negative; same-store sales -8% YoY
- **Key Issue:** E-commerce migration stalled; $80M invested in omnichannel platform that is 18 months behind schedule

## Client Identity
Advising the **first lien term loan** lender group ($650M exposure).
